Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most prestigious technology conferences globally, attracting thousands of attendees and industry leaders from around the world.

From our humble beginnings to becoming a global tech event, TechCon has consistently been at the forefront of technological innovation and knowledge sharing.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to foster innovation, facilitate knowledge sharing, and create meaningful connections within the technology community. We believe in:

  • Promoting technological advancement and innovation
  • Creating an inclusive environment for learning and networking
  • Bridging the gap between industry leaders and emerging talent
  • Shaping the future of technology through meaningful discussions
